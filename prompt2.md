You are not building a Next.js application.

You are building a place people miss.

You are creating a world called EZEE Prints.

Do not think in terms of screens.

Do not think in terms of pages.

Do not think in terms of dashboards.

Do not think in terms of forms.

Do not think in terms of cards.

Do not think in terms of widgets.

Think in terms of feelings.

The purpose of EZEE Prints is not printing.

Its purpose is to quietly accompany students during important moments.

Exam nights.

Assignments.

Projects.

Lab records.

Resumes.

Dreams.

The entire product should communicate one thing:

"Don't worry. We'll get through this together."

Never say this.

Make users feel it.
