You are not building a history page.

You are building a personal library of memories.

Do not create tables.

Do not create cards.

Do not create archives.

Do not create folders.

Do not create order history.

Do not create dashboards.

This experience should answer one question:

"Who was I when I printed this?"

PHILOSOPHY

Every printed document represents a moment in someone's life.

Not an order.

Not a transaction.

A memory.

A struggle.

A dream.

A chapter.

The purpose of this experience is not organization.

It is remembrance.

Users should feel:

"I forgot this happened."

Never say it.

Make them feel it.

EZI

Adding bookmarks.

Reading old notes.
