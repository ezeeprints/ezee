After hours

Curious minds ask

Tap a sticky note to read the answer.

How fast can I get my prints? +

Most jobs are ready in 8–15 minutes. You'll get a ping the moment your stack is warm and waiting at the counter.

Which file formats can I upload? +

PDFs, Word docs, slides, and plain images (JPG/PNG). Drop in as many as you like — Ezi keeps them in order.

How do I pay? +

Pay right in the app with UPI, cards, or wallets before you arrive. Walk in, collect, and you're done — no fumbling for change.

Is my data kept private? +

Always. Files are encrypted, shared only with the shop you choose, and auto-deleted after your job is printed and collected.

The nook's still warm and the lamp's still on. Bring your notes — Ezi will handle the rest.
